Who are we?
We are a small team of Roblox players that joined between 2012 and 2016. We noticed that Roblox is an everchanging Ship Of Theseus and nowadays the platform isn't even close to what it used to be; Sure, there's Experiences that try to emulate how it was, but it's not truly the same thing as back in the day. Maybe at some point Roblox Corporation threw away the essence of their videogame platform for profit, or nostalgia is just putting a rosy filter on the same as it ever was. Whichever is the case, we decided that Old Roblox should be preserved and launched this website, where we'll try to archive the most we can of the early days of a platform that dominates the market of its user-generated content and monetizes it in many horrifying ways turning it into a digital sweatshop.Â
